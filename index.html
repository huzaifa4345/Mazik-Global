Our Services

ERP and CRM Implementation

Mazik Global offers implementation services for Microsoft Dynamics 365, including Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Customer Relationship Management (CRM) solutions. These implementations help businesses streamline their operations, improve customer relationships, and drive growth.

Azure Cloud Solutions

Mazik Global helps businesses migrate to and leverage the Microsoft Azure cloud platform. This includes cloud infrastructure setup, migration services, and cloud management to ensure optimal performance and cost-efficiency.

MazikCare

This is a healthcare management solution built on Microsoft Dynamics 365. MazikCare offers modules for patient engagement, care coordination, and health analytics, designed to improve the quality of care and operational efficiency for healthcare providers.
